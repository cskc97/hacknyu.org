About

HackNYU is a 48-hour hackathon taking place this March 23–25th, hosted at NYU locations around the world. Come to code, learn, and win prizes. This year, we have four hack categories for you to build for: Accessibility & Assistive Technology, Education Technology, Healthcare, and Sustainability & Social Impact. HackNYU is free, and made possible thanks to our wonderful sponsors and volunteers.
